    About Me

    With a background in engineering and a strong analytical mindset, I have transitioned into the world of data analytics, where I transform raw data into meaningful insights. As a Data Analyst, I thrive on uncovering patterns, solving complex problems, and driving data-driven decisions that create real impact.

    My expertise includes data visualization, SQL, Power BI, Excel, and Python, enabling me to analyze large datasets efficiently and present insights in a clear, compelling manner. I am passionate about turning numbers into narratives that help businesses make informed decisions.
